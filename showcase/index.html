Core Capabilities

What Makes Securitix Different

Not just one biometric — three tiers working together, all secured with military-grade encryption.

👁️

Iris Detection

MediaPipe Face Mesh extracts 28 iris geometry features — eye shape ratios, inter-iris distance, and pupil positioning. Cosine similarity matching at 88% threshold.

Tier 1 · MediaPipe · OpenCV
🎤

Voice Recognition

Records your passphrase, extracts 13-coefficient MFCC features, and compares using Dynamic Time Warping. Two samples averaged during enrollment for stability.

Tier 2 · librosa · DTW
✋

Gesture Recognition

MediaPipe Hands tracks 21 3D landmarks, finger angles, and inter-finger distances — 83 features total. Your unique hand gesture becomes your key.

Tier 3 · MediaPipe · 83 Features
🔐

AES Encryption

All biometric templates encrypted at rest using Fernet (AES-128-CBC + HMAC-SHA256). Keys are machine-bound — data can't be decrypted on another computer.

Fernet · Machine-Bound Keys
🫣

Liveness Detection

Blink detection via Eye Aspect Ratio variance prevents spoofing with photos or videos. No blinks detected = rejected as non-live.

Anti-Spoofing · EAR Variance
🎯

Smart Matching

Discrimination margin ensures the best match is significantly better than the 2nd best — preventing misidentification when users have similar biometrics.

Margin Check · Multi-Tier Fusion
Authentication Flow

How It Works

Two phases — enroll once, authenticate anytime. All three tiers must match the same user.

📝 Enrollment

1

Enter Username

Alphanumeric + underscore only. Path traversal attacks are blocked.

2

Iris Capture

50 frames captured with blink-based liveness verification.

3

Voice Recording ×2

Passphrase recorded twice and averaged for a stable voice template.

4

Gesture Capture

30 frames of your unique hand gesture captured with landmark tracking.

5

Encrypt & Store

All features encrypted (AES-128) and saved locally. No raw images stored.

🔓 Authentication

1

Auto-Identify

No username needed. The system scans against ALL enrolled users.

2

3-Tier Biometric Scan

Iris → Voice → Gesture. Each tier compared via threshold + margin check.

3

Identity Fusion

All 3 tiers must pass AND match the same user. Otherwise → denied.
