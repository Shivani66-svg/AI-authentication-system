System Flow

How Authentication Works

1

Enter Username

Alphanumeric + underscore only. Dangerous names and path traversal attempts are auto-blocked.

2

Iris Capture with Liveness

50 frames captured. You must blink naturally — photos and videos are rejected by the EAR variance check.

3

Voice Recording ×2

Your passphrase is recorded twice with a countdown. Both samples are averaged for a stable MFCC template.

4

Gesture Capture

30 frames of your unique hand gesture. 83 features extracted — landmark coords, angles, and fingertip distances.

✓

Encrypt & Store

All features AES-128 encrypted with a machine-bound key. No raw images or audio saved — ever.

1

No Username Needed

The system automatically identifies you by scanning against ALL enrolled users in the database.

2

Iris + Liveness Check

Blink detection first, then iris geometry compared against all stored templates with threshold + margin checks.

3

Voice DTW Matching

Live passphrase compared using Dynamic Time Warping. Must be within DTW distance ≤ 45.0 from your template.

4

Gesture Verification

Your hand gesture compared with cosine similarity. Must reach 90% match with discrimination margin enforced.

✓

Identity Fusion

All 3 tiers must pass AND all must identify the SAME user. Any mismatch = access denied + audit log entry.
